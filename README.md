# 🏕️ Rover E-Logbook

> A digital logbook platform for Rover Scouts to document, share, and celebrate their scouting journey.

---

## 📖 About The Project

Rover Scouts are adult members of the scouting movement, and tradition requires every Rover to maintain a **logbook** documenting their crew meetings, events, and meaningful experiences. However, most Rovers juggle full-time jobs, studies, and other responsibilities — making it impractical to carry a physical logbook everywhere they go.

**Rover E-Logbook** modernizes this tradition by bringing it online. It's a clean, dashboard-driven web platform where Rovers can record their scouting journey digitally, share approved entries publicly, and reflect on their personal growth — all from their phone or laptop.

---

## ✨ Features

### 👤 Personalized Rover Profiles
Each Rover gets a dedicated profile containing their basic information — name, troop (selected from a predefined list), date of birth, address, and contact details — serving as the home base of their digital logbook.

### 📝 Logbook Entries
Rovers can compose blog-style posts for two categories:
- **Crew Meetings** — Weekly or routine gatherings
- **Events** — Camps, hikes, service projects, and special activities

Entries support rich text and allow a limited number of image attachments to keep storage efficient.

### ✅ Approval Workflow
Submitted posts are routed to an authorized reviewer (e.g., a **Rover Scout Leader**) who verifies the content before it goes live. Only approved posts appear publicly on the Rover's profile — preserving the integrity of the logbook tradition.

### 📊 Dashboard-Style Profile View
Once approved, posts populate a dynamic dashboard featuring:
- Total **crew meetings** attended
- Total **events** participated in
- Number of **posts published**
- **Badges** earned along the way

### 📰 Articles Section
Beyond logged events, each Rover can publish **articles** — personal reflections on topics, life lessons, or subjects they care about. This space encourages deeper thinking and helps Rovers process what they're learning through scouting.

### 🌐 Public Profiles
Approved logs and articles are publicly viewable, so friends, family, fellow Rovers, and the wider scouting community can follow along on each Rover's journey.

---

## 👥 User Roles

| Role | Permissions |
|------|-------------|
| **Rover** | Create profile, submit posts, write articles, view own dashboard |
| **Rover Scout Leader** | Review and approve/reject submitted posts |
| **Public Visitor** | View approved profiles, posts, and articles |
